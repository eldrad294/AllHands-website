What is AllHands?

AllHands is a full-stack, cross-platform marketplace app for local service booking. Service providers list what they offer — with pricing, location, and availability. Clients discover, book, and pay — all in one place.

AllHands delivers a reliable booking experience across iOS, Android, and web.

Platform Features

Everything you need to connect, book, and manage local services — in one platform.

Service Discovery

Browse and search services by location, category, and availability using real-time geolocation.

Booking Management

Full booking lifecycle — open, confirm, cancel — with real-time status tracking for both parties.

Secure Payments

Save your payment method and pay seamlessly. Your card details are handled securely and never stored directly by AllHands.

User Profiles

Rich provider profiles with photo, bio, and service listings. Verified accounts via email authentication.

Role-Based Experience

Distinct client and provider views from a single account — tailored UI and colour coding for each perspective.

Cross-Platform

iOS, Android, and web — a consistent experience everywhere.

How It Works

Getting started is straightforward — whether you're looking for a service or offering one.

For Clients

1

Create an Account

Sign up with your email, verify with a 6-digit code, and complete your profile.

2

Find a Service

Search by location or category, browse provider profiles, pricing, and availability.

3

Book Your Appointment

Confirm your booking and track your appointment — AllHands is free to use.

For Providers

1

List Your Service

Add your service with location, pricing, availability windows, and category tags.

2

Receive Bookings

Clients request your service; review and confirm or decline each booking.

3

Connect and Deliver

Connect with your client and deliver your service — AllHands is completely free to use.
