Account Closure & Data Deletion

Effective date: April 12, 2026

1. Overview

You have the right to close your AllHands account and request deletion of all personal data we hold about you. This page explains what happens when you make that request, what data is removed, what is legally required to be retained, and how to submit a request.

2. What Gets Deleted

Upon processing a deletion request, the following data associated with your account will be permanently removed:

  • Profile data: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and profile picture.
  • Booking history: All booking records created under your account.
  • Service listings: Any services you listed as a provider, including descriptions, pricing, and location data.
  • Saved addresses: Any service or location data associated with your account.
  • Payment methods: Your stored payment method tokens are removed from our payment processor. We will no longer be able to charge you.
  • Authentication data: All active session tokens are invalidated and hashed credentials are deleted.
  • Notification preferences: All communication settings and preferences linked to your account.

3. What We Retain

Certain records must be retained after account deletion for legal and financial compliance purposes:

  • Audit logs: Anonymised payment operation logs are retained for financial integrity and dispute resolution. These records do not contain personally identifiable information after deletion.
  • Legal obligations: Any data we are required to retain under applicable law (including financial regulations) will be kept for the minimum period required and then securely deleted.

No retained records will be used for any purpose other than the legal or compliance obligation for which they are kept.

4. Effect on Active Bookings

Before submitting a deletion request, please ensure the following:

  • Cancel or complete any active or pending bookings through the app.
  • Resolve any outstanding payments — pending charges at the time of deletion will still be processed.
  • Service providers should ensure no upcoming client bookings remain open.

We recommend using the app to settle all activity before requesting deletion.

5. How to Request Deletion

To request account closure and data deletion, send an email to allhands.backend@gmail.com with the following details:

  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • Body: Include the email address registered to your AllHands account.

Requests must be sent from the email address registered to your account. Requests from a different address cannot be verified and will not be actioned.

6. Processing Timeframe

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email to your registered address once the deletion is complete.

If you do not receive a confirmation within 30 days, please follow up at the email address above.

7. Reactivation

Deleted accounts cannot be reactivated. Once your account is closed and your data is removed, the action is permanent and irreversible. If you wish to use AllHands in the future, you will need to create a new account.
